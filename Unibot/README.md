Unibot is a demo version of a task-oriented dialogue system that helps Greek speaking college students with requests related to a course of their interest. It is designed to fulfill different requests based on the type of information a student desires to retrieve about a specific course of their semester. The system can complete three different main tasks: offer information about the detailed schedule of a course’s class (day, time, professor, classroom), offer a summary of a course’s subject and carry through with both these tasks at once.
